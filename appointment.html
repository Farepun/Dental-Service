Dental Crowns

A crown is a cap placed over a tooth that has been damaged or weakened, restoring its shape, size, and strength. Crowns are typically made of porcelain, metal, or a combination of both.

Dental Fillings

A dental filling is used to restore a tooth that has been affected by decay. The decayed part of the tooth is removed and then filled with materials like composite resin, amalgam, or gold to restore the tooth's shape and function.
